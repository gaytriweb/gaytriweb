ABOUT THE CAFE

The Cafe was founded in 1989 by Mr. Lalwani in Raipur with nothing but passion and Rs.15,000. He took his savings and started the cafe. Initially, we just served one range of coffee but now we have expanded to four ranges of coffee and other items as well.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Mr. Dhanjay Lalwani

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.

Address: Jaisur Market, Raipur
